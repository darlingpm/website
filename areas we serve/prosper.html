Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical rent range for a 3-bedroom house in Prosper?
2-bedroom homes typically rent for $1,900–$2,300/month, while 3-bedroom single-family homes range from $2,500–$3,200/month depending on condition, location within Prosper, and proximity to employment centers. Premium neighborhoods like Windsong Ranch and Star Trail command the higher end.
How much does it cost to use Darling Property Management?
Our full management fee is 10% of monthly rent — right in line with the DFW average of 10–12%. Tenant placement is 75% of the first month's rent ($1,000 minimum). There are no fees while your property is vacant. For leasing-only services, the fee is 100% of the first month's rent.
Why is Prosper such a strong rental market?
Prosper combines three powerful factors: rapid population growth (one of the fastest in Texas), premium new construction that attracts high-income families, and a top-rated school district. The result is strong demand for quality single-family rentals from professional, stable tenants who prioritize education and lifestyle.
Do you handle maintenance emergencies?
Yes. We have 24/7 emergency response for situations like water damage, HVAC failure, security breaches, and no utilities. Our vendor network responds within 1 hour for true emergencies. Routine maintenance is scheduled during business hours and typically completed within 3–7 business days.
How often will I get an owner report?
You'll get a detailed monthly report that includes rent collected, maintenance itemized by cost and category, tenant status, lease expiration dates, and your net disbursement. We also provide an annual property review and tax-ready financial summary.
What's your tenant screening process?
We pull credit reports (TransUnion/Equifax), criminal background checks, and eviction history for every applicant. We verify employment, check rental references, and require income ≥ 3x monthly rent. We apply the same criteria to every applicant to ensure Fair Housing compliance.
