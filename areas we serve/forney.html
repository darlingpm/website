What Makes Forney Special

Among Texas's Fastest-Growing Cities

Forney ranks in the top 5 fastest-growing cities in Texas by percentage growth. This trajectory creates sustained demand and reliable property appreciation.

Affordable New Construction Below Market

Master-planned communities like Windmill Farms and Devonshire offer new homes under $360K median—below neighboring Rockwall and Rowlett—with lower maintenance burden.

I-80 Corridor Direct Dallas Access

30-minute commute to Dallas via US-80. Price-sensitive professionals choose Forney for affordability while maintaining Dallas employment. Strong renter demand.

Forney ISD Growth Projections

Forney ISD is expanding to serve rapid population growth. Long-term family demand will sustain above-market rents as schools build reputation.

Minimal Institutional Competition

Unlike Frisco, Plano, or McKinney, Forney is primarily individual investors. Less competition means higher margins and better deal flow for operators like us.

Collin County's Past Is Kaufman County's Future

Forney's growth trajectory mirrors Collin County 15 years ago. Early operators who positioned in outskirts before institutional capital arrived captured exceptional appreciation.

Frequently Asked

What are typical rent ranges in Forney?
Forney's 2-bedroom homes rent for $1,450–$1,650/month, and 3-bedrooms for $1,700–$2,050/month depending on age, condition, and community. New construction typically rents 10% higher than older homes.
How much does property management cost?
Our full management fee is 10% of monthly rent — right in line with the DFW average of 10–12%. Tenant placement is 75% of the first month's rent ($1,000 minimum). There are no fees while your property is vacant. For leasing-only services, the fee is 100% of the first month's rent.
Why is Forney one of the fastest-growing cities in Texas?
Forney offers affordable new construction, I-80 commute access, quality schools, and room to grow—all at a lower price point than northern DFW suburbs. This affordability coupled with Dallas commuter access drives sustained migration.
How long does it take to find a tenant in Forney?
Average days on market in Forney is 60 days. Strong commuter demand and new home appeal help fill properties quickly. Professional photography, compelling copy, and syndication to 30+ platforms reduce this time by 15–25%.
Are new construction rentals easier to manage?
Yes. New homes have fewer maintenance issues during the first few years, lower turnover (tenants stay longer in new construction), and command 5–10% higher rents than comparable older homes.
