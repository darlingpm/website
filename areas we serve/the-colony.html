Why The Colony Attracts Smart Investors

The Colony blends lifestyle appeal with economic growth — Lewisville Lake access, major commercial development, and proximity to Frisco's job corridor create an exceptional investment backdrop.

  • Lewisville Lake waterfront drives lifestyle premium and appeals to long-term renters seeking recreation and outdoor access
  • Grandscape development — the largest mixed-use development in DFW — brings new retail, hospitality, and employment to the area
  • Nebraska Furniture Mart and surrounding retail create consistent local employment and foot traffic
  • Affordable entry vs. Frisco and Plano allows investors to build portfolio scale while remaining in the same growth corridor
  • DART bus connectivity and proximity to Frisco's corporate corridor keep renters flowing from nearby job centers
  • Lewisville ISD schools and family-friendly amenities attract longer-lease family tenants

Common Questions

The Colony Property Management FAQs

How much does property management cost in The Colony?
Our full management fee is 10% of monthly rent — right in line with the DFW average of 10–12%. Tenant placement is 75% of the first month's rent ($1,000 minimum). There are no fees while your property is vacant. For leasing-only services, the fee is 100% of the first month's rent.
What's the typical rent range for a 2-bed or 3-bed in The Colony?
A 2-bed typically rents $1,450–$1,650 depending on location and condition. A 3-bed ranges $1,750–$2,150. Properties with lake views or access command the higher end. We conduct a free rental analysis for every property to determine market-rate positioning.
How do you find tenants in The Colony?
We syndicate every listing to Zillow, Apartments.com, Zumper, Realtor.com, and PadMapper in 24 hours. Our in-house photography and professional listing copy (written by our team) generate 3–4x more inquiries than average. We also leverage our network of corporate relocation brokers. Screening is rigorous and fair — we apply consistent criteria to every applicant.
How fast can you lease my vacant property in The Colony?
Our average time to lease in The Colony is 56 days. Our fastest placements happen in 21 days. Speed depends on pricing, condition, and location. We'll give you an honest assessment during the onboarding consultation.
Do you handle emergency maintenance in The Colony?
Yes. We have a vetted vendor network with 24/7 emergency response for water leaks, HVAC failures, electrical hazards, and security issues. Emergencies are dispatched within 1 hour. Routine repairs are scheduled within 3–7 business days and batched to control costs.
What if a tenant doesn't pay rent in The Colony?
We send an automated reminder 3 days before the due date, then follow a structured delinquency protocol. We draft late-fee notices, formal demands, and — if necessary — begin eviction filings in Justice of the Peace court. We never shut off utilities or lock out tenants; we follow Texas Property Code §92.008 precisely. Legal consultation is recommended for contested evictions.
