Why Invest Here

Why Wylie is a smart bet for rental investors

Wylie combines DFW growth exposure with affordability and lifestyle appeal that attracts both renters and resale buyers.

  • Best rent-to-price ratio in Collin County: Entry prices 15–20% below Plano/Frisco with equivalent tenant quality. Superior cap rates.
  • New construction + strong population growth: DFW spillover driving population boom. Newer housing stock means lower maintenance costs and longer replacement cycles.
  • Lake Lavon lifestyle: Unique amenity in North Texas. Attracts outdoor-minded renters, families, and recreationalists. Differentiates Wylie from neighbors.
  • Top-rated Wylie ISD: Strong schools attract families as owner-occupants and long-term renters. School performance drives property values.
  • US-78 corridor access: Direct route to Rockwall, Garland, and Mesquite employment. Growing retail/industrial corridor (Firewheel Town Center).
  • Rent growth outpacing supply: New construction can't keep up with demand. Limited multifamily = stable single-family rental economics.

Common Questions

Wylie property management FAQ

What's the typical rent for a 2-bed or 3-bed in Wylie?
Based on current market data (Mar 2026): A 2-bedroom home in Wylie typically rents for $1,450–$1,650/month. A 3-bedroom (the most popular) runs $1,850–$2,200/month depending on location and amenities. Newer Maxwell Creek properties command a slight premium; older Woodbridge homes offer entry-level pricing. We'll provide a specific analysis for your property free of charge.
How long does it take to lease a vacant property?
Wylie's average market time is 56 days from listing to signed lease. With our professional photos, Innago syndication to 15+ major platforms, and targeted tenant screening, we typically fill properties in 40–55 days. Wylie's growth trajectory keeps demand strong. We keep you updated weekly with showing activity and interest.
What are your management fees?
Our full management fee is 10% of monthly rent — right in line with the DFW average of 10–12%. Tenant placement is 75% of the first month's rent ($1,000 minimum). There are no fees while your property is vacant. For leasing-only services, the fee is 100% of the first month's rent. No hidden fees, no surprises.
Do you handle emergency maintenance calls 24/7?
Yes. Any emergency (burst pipe, no heat, electrical hazard, break-in) is responded to within 1 hour by our preferred vendor network. Non-emergencies are scheduled within 24 hours. All work orders are logged in our system, and you get monthly reports. We prioritize cost control — vendors compete for our volume and understand our quality standards.
How do you screen tenants in Wylie?
We pull credit, criminal, and eviction reports through TransUnion/Equifax. Our screening criteria require income ≥3x monthly rent, a credit score of ≥650, zero evictions in the past 5 years, and positive rental references. We apply these standards identically to all applicants — Fair Housing compliant, documented, no exceptions. You'll know your tenant is vetted before lease signing.
When will I get paid, and what's included in the monthly report?
Rent is collected on the 1st of each month and disbursed by the 15th via Baselane. Your monthly report includes rent collected, itemized maintenance costs, net cash flow, tenant status, lease expiration dates, vacancy status, and year-over-year comparisons. Reports are delivered in writing and available in our online portal 24/7. We also conduct annual rent reviews to keep your property competitive.
