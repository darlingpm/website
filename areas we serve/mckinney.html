Why Invest Here

Why McKinney is a smart bet for rental investors

McKinney offers something rare in DFW — a nationally recognized brand, a walkable historic core, and some of the best master-planned communities in Texas, all in a city that's still growing fast enough to reward early investors.

  • #1 Best Place to Live in America: Named by Money Magazine, McKinney's national brand recognition generates continuous inbound migration — new residents who need rental housing while they explore the area and decide where to buy.
  • Historic Downtown Square: One of the best-preserved downtowns in Texas, featuring boutique retail, restaurants, art galleries, and community events. Homes near the square command premium rents from tenants who value walkability and character.
  • Craig Ranch & TPC Golf Course: This premium master-planned community features luxury homes, the TPC Craig Ranch golf course (host of the CJ Cup Byron Nelson PGA Tour event), and resort-style amenities. Rentals here command $2,700+/month.
  • Stonebridge Ranch: One of the largest and most established master-planned communities in Texas with over 5,000 homes, multiple parks, pools, and trails. Families rent here for years due to the strong community and McKinney ISD schools.
  • McKinney ISD: Multiple 10/10 GreatSchools-rated schools across the district draw family renters who prioritize education and tend to sign longer leases.
  • Growth Corridor: US-75 and SH-121 access puts McKinney within 30 minutes of Plano, Frisco, and Dallas job centers. New developments like Trinity Falls and Painted Tree continue to expand the city northward, attracting builders and renters alike.
