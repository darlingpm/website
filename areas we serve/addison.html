Why Invest Here

Why Addison is a smart bet for rental investors

Addison packs more economic punch per square mile than almost any city in DFW. Small footprint, massive rental demand.

  • 61% Renter Rate: With nearly two-thirds of residents renting, Addison's tenant pool is deep and consistently replenishing — vacancy is rare and turnover doesn't hurt you for long.
  • 170+ Restaurants & Entertainment: Addison hosts more restaurants per capita than almost any city in the US. Walkability and lifestyle appeal attract professional renters willing to pay premium rents for convenience.
  • Addison Circle Walkability: One of DFW's few truly walkable urban districts. Tenants pay a premium for the ability to walk to dining, shopping, and parks from their front door.
  • Upscale Demographics: High-income professionals and executives make up a disproportionate share of Addison renters — expect stronger credit profiles, lower maintenance costs, and fewer collection issues.
  • Tollway Employment Corridor: Direct access to the Dallas North Tollway places Addison renters minutes from major corporate employers in Plano, Frisco, and Las Colinas.
  • Vitruvian Park Appeal: The 11-acre Vitruvian Park is a year-round amenity that draws tenants seeking outdoor lifestyle access in an urban setting — a rare combination that commands above-market rents.
