Why Invest Here

Why Garland is a smart bet for rental investors

Garland is the cash-flow capital of North Dallas. Lower acquisition costs, strong renter demand, and exceptional long-term appreciation make it one of the most overlooked opportunities in the DFW Metroplex.

  • Strongest Price-to-Rent Ratio in North Dallas: A $300K median home price with $2,095/mo 3-bed rents makes Garland one of the best cash-flow markets in DFW — significantly better ratios than Plano, Frisco, or Richardson.
  • 119% Appreciation (10-Year): Garland homes have appreciated 119% over the past decade, placing it in the top 20% nationally for long-term real estate appreciation (NeighborhoodScout).
  • DART Light Rail + Bus Rapid Transit: Multiple DART stations connect Garland directly to downtown Dallas and Richardson's Telecom Corridor, driving consistent commuter renter demand.
  • Firewheel Town Center: This major retail and entertainment hub anchors North Garland and increases desirability — and rents — in surrounding neighborhoods like Firewheel and Club Hill.
  • Diverse Housing Stock: 69% single-family homes, 23% apartments, and a growing mix of townhomes give investors multiple entry points at every budget level.
  • Major Employers Nearby: ABB, Sasol, L-3 Technologies, plus direct adjacency to Richardson's Telecom Corridor (130,000+ jobs) create stable, employment-driven renter demand.
